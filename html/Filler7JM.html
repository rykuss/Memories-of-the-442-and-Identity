Memories of the 442 and Identity

Now that you’re back home it is time for you to move away from the war and establish your career path. You take advantage of the Army’s funds and go back to school to pursue a degree. You now know exactly what it takes for you to commit to something and do it right. You often think of your pals who perished right next to you and the dreams that they had lost on the battlefield. This gives you strength and endurance to push forward for yourself and your family.
