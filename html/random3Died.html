Memories of the 442 and Identity

You stand in a daze, when suddenly your division commander gives you a kick, ordering you to charge forward. Startled, you charge forward as commanded, and shout a war cry. You feel as if there is an invisible force, pushing you forward regardless of the pain you feel. As you take cover behind a nearby tree trunk, you see an enemy soldier, and he fires a shot into your shoulder. You fall down, and are hit again by the explosion of an enemy grenade. Your vision fades to black, as you hear your allies call for a medic, but slowly your consciousness begins to fade...You've perished
