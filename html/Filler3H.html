Memories of the 442 and Identity

You are sent off to Camp Shelby, Mississippi in March where you do well. You made it up the ranks while training at Camp Shelby. Off to Anzio you go. Italy’s not going to be pretty this time around but defending your country sure will be. Don’t let it get to you that General Dahlquist steals away the recognition of you and your pals.
